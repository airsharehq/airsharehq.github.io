What makes it special

Everything you need for seamless, secure file sharing on your local network.

Peer-to-Peer Direct

100% peer-to-peer transfers. No middleman, no cloud servers. Your files never leave your local network.

Lightning Fast QUIC

Modern QUIC protocol with multiplexed streams. Faster than TCP, optimized for large files.

TLS 1.3 Encrypted

End-to-end encryption with TLS 1.3. SHA-256 integrity verification on every file.

Auto Discovery

mDNS finds devices automatically. No manual IP entry. No setup. Just works.
