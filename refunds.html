Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how subscriptions and cancellations work for AirShare paid plans.

1. Free plan

The Free plan is available at no cost.

2. Subscriptions

  • Paid plans renew automatically until you cancel.
  • You can cancel at any time; your access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

3. Refunds

All purchases are non-refundable. Please evaluate the Free plan before upgrading to a paid plan.

4. How to cancel

You can manage or cancel your subscription via the Paddle billing portal link provided in your receipt or by contacting info@trayairshare.com.

5. Contact

Billing questions: info@trayairshare.com.
