Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The Free plan lets you test AirShare fully. When you're ready for unlimited transfers, upgrade to Pro.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other payment methods through our secure payment provider Dodo Payments.

Can I upgrade from Yearly to Lifetime?

Yes! Contact us at info@tryairshare.com and we'll credit your yearly purchase towards the lifetime license.

What's your refund policy?

All sales are final. We do not offer refunds for licenses, except where required by law. Please try the free version first. See our refund policy.

Can I use Pro on multiple devices?

Yes! One license works on all your devices (Mac, Windows, Linux), but can only be activated once. After activation, it works across all your devices. Contact info@tryairshare.com if you need to transfer your license.

Do you offer team/business pricing?

Yes! Contact us at info@tryairshare.com for volume licensing.

What happens when my yearly license expires?

You'll continue to use the app, but will revert to Free plan limits. You can renew anytime to restore Pro features.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed securely through Dodo Payments, a trusted merchant of record. We never see or store your payment details.
