Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information AirShare processes when you use our website and apps, and how we use it. AirShare is designed to work locally and minimize data collection.

1. Data we do not collect by default

  • No account is required to use AirShare.
  • No file contents are sent to our servers during local (LAN) transfers.
  • No contact lists or precise location data are collected.

2. Data we may process

  • Website hosting: Downloads are served via GitHub Pages and GitHub Releases. GitHub may log standard technical data (IP address, user agent) in accordance with their policies.
  • Payments: If you purchase a paid plan, billing data is processed by Paddle. We receive limited metadata necessary for license fulfillment (subscription status, plan).
  • Support: If you email us at info@trayairshare.com, we process the information you provide to respond.

3. Peer-to-peer transfers

Local transfers occur directly between devices on your network using QUIC + mDNS. When using an encrypted relay for out-of-LAN connectivity, traffic is end-to-end encrypted; relay operators may see limited operational metadata (timestamps, IPs) but cannot read file contents.

4. Cookies

We do not set our own cookies. GitHub Pages and Paddle may set strictly necessary cookies for security, session handling and payments. We do not use third‑party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

5. Data retention

  • Billing metadata is retained for the period required by tax/accounting laws.
  • Support emails are retained for reasonable periods to handle follow-ups.

6. Your rights (GDPR/EU)

Where applicable, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability. To exercise rights, contact info@trayairshare.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Security

We use industry-standard protections (TLS, encryption in transit). AirShare LAN transfers are peer-to-peer; keep your devices updated and protected with strong OS-level security.

8. Children

AirShare is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we processed data from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

9. Changes

We may revise this Policy. We will update the “Last updated” date and, when material, provide additional notice.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: info@trayairshare.com.
