Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AirShare (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and processes personal data when you use our application, website, and related services (“Services”).

1. Data we collect

  • License data: email address and transaction identifiers from Paddle to issue and validate licenses (one-time 1-year or lifetime). We do not collect payment card details — Paddle is the Merchant of Record.
  • Support data: information you voluntarily provide when contacting us (e.g., email content, crash logs you send).
  • Usage data (optional): if enabled, basic telemetry for diagnostics (e.g., app version, OS type). We aim to keep this minimal and anonymous.

2. How we use data

  • To issue, validate, and manage licenses.
  • To provide support and improve the Services.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records via Paddle).

3. Legal bases

We process personal data based on performance of a contract (providing the Software), legitimate interests (fraud prevention, product improvement), and legal obligations (tax/accounting).

4. Sharing

  • Paddle (billing, tax, invoices). Paddle privacy terms apply when you interact with Paddle.
  • Infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting for our website/API) under data processing agreements.
  • We do not sell personal data.

5. Data retention

We retain licensing and transaction reference data as long as necessary for license validation, support, and legal obligations. Support emails are kept as needed to handle your request.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. Contact us at info@trayairshare.com. If we rely on your consent for optional telemetry, you can withdraw it in-app.

8. International transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

9. Children

AirShare is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the latest version on this page with the effective date below.

11. Contact

Questions or requests? Email info@trayairshare.com.
